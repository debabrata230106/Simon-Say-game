Know About Simon Says

Simon Says is a classic memory game where players repeat sequences of colors and sounds. The challenge increases as the sequence grows longer. Stay sharp and test your memory!

Game Rules

  • Click "Start Game" to begin.
  • Watch the sequence of colors and sounds carefully.
  • Repeat the exact sequence by clicking the buttons.
  • If you succeed, the sequence gets longer.
  • One mistake ends the round — try again!
